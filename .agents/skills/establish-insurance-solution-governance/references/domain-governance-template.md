# Domain Governance Reference Template

Create one peer reference for each newly supported insurance domain. Replace
every bracketed placeholder and obtain domain-owner review before treating its
controls as approved.

## Domain identity

- Domain: `[domain]`
- Accountable steward role: `[role]`
- Applicable lines of business: `[LOB list]`
- Jurisdictions: `[jurisdictions or not applicable]`
- Source product/module/version scope: `[scope]`

## Scope dimensions

List the business processes, lifecycle intervals, objects, measures, and grains
that define the domain. State exclusions explicitly.

## Boundary decisions

For every adjacent domain, record which domain owns the concept and which owns
only the relationship or role. Identify shared identifiers and cross-domain
approval responsibilities.

## Evidence and privacy controls

List permitted structural/profile evidence, prohibited evidence, minimization
rules, prompt/retrieval eligibility, retention, and Privacy Steward triggers.

## Mandatory reviews

Define Domain Steward, Data Architect, Privacy Steward, and specialist-review
triggers for semantics, identifiers, relationships, grain, measures, extensions,
low confidence, and contradictions.

## Evaluation coverage

Define representative positive, ambiguous, contradictory, unsupported, privacy,
and version-mismatch cases. Name metrics, thresholds, owners, and evidence
sources without inventing approval.

## Completion evidence

Require version, effective date, owner, reviewer, approved scope and exclusions,
boundary decisions, evidence policy, review rules, labelled-set coverage,
unresolved items, and superseded reference where applicable.
