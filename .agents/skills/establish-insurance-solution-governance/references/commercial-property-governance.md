# Commercial Property Governance Reference

Use this reference for commercial property and property-bearing business-owner
products.

## Scope dimensions

Record jurisdiction, insured organization, premises and building grain,
occupancy, business activity, construction, protection, stock/equipment,
property coverage, valuation basis, business-interruption basis, and indemnity
period.

## Boundary decisions

- Policy owns contracts and terms; Commercial Property owns premises and exposure specializations.
- Party owns organizations; the product module owns only insured-business roles.
- Shared Location owns location identity; this module owns site risk characteristics.
- Claims owns losses and financials; the module owns property-damage and interruption classifications.

## Evidence and mandatory review

Permit structural metadata and minimized categorical profiles. Protect precise
site security, hazard, valuation, and business-sensitive operational evidence.
Require Domain Steward review for occupancy, construction, valuation, and
business-interruption semantics; Data Architect review for site/building grain;
Privacy or Security review for precise location and protection evidence.

## Evaluation coverage

Test single- and multi-location risks, building/stock/equipment separation,
business interruption, mixed occupancy, ambiguous valuation, unsupported
engineering fields, and source-version mismatch.
