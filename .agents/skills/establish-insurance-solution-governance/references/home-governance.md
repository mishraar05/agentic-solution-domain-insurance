# Home Governance Reference

Use this reference for homeowners, renters/condo, and dwelling-fire ontology
and recommendation work.

## Scope dimensions

Record jurisdiction, product form, ownership/tenure, occupancy, building and
contents scope, construction characteristics, insured location, coverage form,
limits, deductibles, and valuation basis. Distinguish building, contents,
valuable item, liability, and alternative-accommodation grains.

## Boundary decisions

- Policy owns the contract; Home owns residential-risk specializations.
- Shared Location owns the location identity; Home owns occupancy and construction meaning.
- Claims owns claim and payment records; Home owns residential peril and damage classifications.
- Party owns residents and insureds; Home records only their governed roles.

## Evidence and mandatory review

Minimize full addresses, resident information, security details, occupancy
answers, and high-value-item descriptions. Require Domain Steward review for
coverage, occupancy, tenure, construction, and valuation semantics; Data
Architect review for building/contents grain; Privacy Steward review for
address or household evidence.

## Evaluation coverage

Test homeowners, renters/condo, dwelling fire, combined and separated
building/contents structures, ambiguous occupancy, unsupported smart-home
fields, privacy routing, and source-version mismatch.
